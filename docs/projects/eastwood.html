Eastwood Village Rehab

Detroit, MI

Overview

Eastwood Village represents Banor's integrated model at scale. Twenty-four vacant properties were acquired through the Detroit Land Bank. Foundation graduates completed 60% of labor. The project created 42 mixed-income units and catalyzed additional private investment on three surrounding blocks.
